Poké Cup 2025 Rules V5.3.1
	

Battle Log Format:
[MSG: (YYYY-MM-DD) (HH:MM:SS)] 

This is a battle between Ash and Team Rocket for the Poké Cup 2025 Finals, however Team Rocket's  battle logs seem to have been tampered with

Analyse the battle logs between Ash and Team Rocket: 

1. The original message is XORed with a secret key.
2. The result of the XOR operation is then encoded using Base64.
